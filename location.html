International House Darwin

iGrow - Location

The Northern Territory is a vast land of many natural wonders. While living at International House Darwin you will get the opportunity to visit, camp and explore many sites, which will give you a true understanding of what makes the Territory unique from the rest of Australia.

 

Some of the trips IHD residents have partaken in over the past year include:

  • Litchfield National Park
  • Berry Springs
  • Katherine Gorge
  • Katherine Hot Springs
  • Edith Falls
  • Kakadu National Park
  • Yellow Waters
  • Ubirr Rock
  • Jumping Crocodile Cruise
  • Crocodylus Park
  • Crocosaurus Cove
  • Territory Wildlife Park
  • Mandorah
  • Sailing in the Darwin Harbour.

Many of the places we take you to can be expensive to access, however IHD offers these experiences either free or at heavily discounted prices to ensure you don't miss out!

Many of the events merge across multiple iGrow categories. To keep up to date and register to attend events, make sure you regularly check the Events at IHD webpage.
