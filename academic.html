iGrow - Academic Development

IHD can help connect you to Charles Darwin University's Academic Language and Learning Success Program (ALLSP), which offers a range of free workshops, aimed at developing the academic skills needed to succeed in university studies at both the undergraduate and postgraduate level.

These workshops are presented on campus and online. They also provide a range of online resources, including archived recordings of workshops and workshop materials. Some of the workshops available include:

  • Strategies and language to confidently participate in your tutorials and on discussion boards
  • Discussion and debating
  • English pronunciation
  • General English language
  • Academic Culture
  • APA Referencing
  • Critical Analysis
  • Editing and Proofreading Skills
  • Effective Reading & Note Making
  • Effective Research Skills 2: Incorporating Source Material
  • Essay Structure & Format
  • Exam Preparation, Revision Strategies & Stress Management
  • Harvard Referencing
  • Interpreting & Planning Assignments

ALLSP also has a new Study Skills website where you can find a range of comprehensive study skills information which should assist you to become a more confident student at Charles Darwin University. The Study Skills website contains information about:

  • Academic assignments
  • Academic writing
  • Referencing
  • Critical thinking
  • Exams
  • Lectures and tutorials
  • Note making
  • Research and reading
  • Managing your studies

IHD encourages all students to attend at least one ALLSP workshop that concentrates on the academic skills you wish to learn about and improve on.

Many of the events merge across multiple iGrow categories. To keep up to date and register to attend events, make sure you regularly check the Events at IHD webpage.
