iGrow - Personal Wellbeing

The Gym offers a modern, fully equipped, airconditioned gymnasium with a comprehensive range of fitness programs, services and memberships to students, staff and club members, seniors and the general public. A range of affordable membership options are available.


With a fully qualified and professional team, you will be assisted in achieving your fitness goals. Let our staff design a personal fitness program tailored to your needs to help you toward 'training for life'.

We offer a range of programs including:

  • personally tailored programs
  • fitness assessments
  • exercise classes for all levels
  • sports specific fitness testing on and off-site
  • sports specific pre- and in-season training programs and advice
  • personal training
  • rehabilitation Services
  • exercise Physiology Clinic

Check out the full list of programs available at our website.

Many of the events merge across multiple iGrow categories. To keep up to date and register to attend events, make sure you regularly check the Events at IHD webpage.
