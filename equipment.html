Equipment Hire

  • Bikes
  • Sporting equipment
  • DVD & Games
  • Kitchen sets
  • Camping and picnic equipment

Bikes

IHD has one of the best commuter bikes available for hire - The Vanmoof No. 5. This bike comes with an internal 3 speed hub, kick brake, internally housed rising lights and an integrated lock.


Bike hire is free and IHD has 10 bikes available.
Head to the office to hire a bike, you will need to sign the Bike Hire Agreement and hand over your Room key as a deposit. Bikes can only be borrowed between the hours of 7am and 10pm.

Kitchen Sets

IHD has basic kitchen kits available for hire. A kitchen kit costs $150.00 for long term residents to purchase and for hire to short term residents (less than 4 weeks) at a cost of $1.50 per day and a bond of $150.00.
To buy or hire a kitchen set contact the IHD Office.

Sets contain:

  • Table knife, fork, dessert spoon and teaspoon
  • Kitchen Knife
  • Plate, bowl, mug and glass
  • 3 saucespans (assorted sizes)
  • Fry pan
  • 3 mixing bowls (assorted sizes)
  • Assorted cooking utensils
  • Cutting board
  • Vegie peeler and grater

Camping and picnic equipment

IHD has a range of eskies, BBQ equipment, tents etc for loan. To arrange a hire of this equipment please contact the IHD Office.
