iGrow - Cultural Experiences

IHD, along with its Resident Leaders, organises events throughout the year to celebrate the many cultures represented within the IHD community.

Our events range from international cuisine cooking nights, celebrating our cultural diversity through Harmony Week and learning about major cultural events by celebrating events such as Holi, St Patricks Day, Ramadan, and Australia Day to name a few.

Many of the events merge across multiple iGrow categories. To keep up to date and register to attend events, make sure you regularly check the Events at IHD webpage.
